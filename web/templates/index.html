  4. Reconnect to your internal services after trust is installed.

Troubleshooting

  • Check the SHA-256 fingerprint before trusting the certificate.
  • Confirm the device date and time are correct.
  • Remove old copies of the root certificate if trust still fails.
  • Some apps use their own trust store and may ignore system roots.
